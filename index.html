ORIGAMI DESIGNS

Some intereting points about camel:

  1. Camel's ears are furry.
  2. Camel can easily across sand because of it's specially designed feet.
  3. When they will find water, they will drink as much as possible.
  4. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  5. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  6. With the exception of rutting males competing for females, camels are very peaceful animals who rarely exhibit aggression.
  7. There are more than 14 million camels in the world – most of them live in the Middle East, Asia and Australia.

Some intereting facts about chameleon:

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder.
  2. Changing color is an important part of communication among Chameleons.
  3. Most Chameleons have prehensile tail that they use to wrap free branches.
  4. Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads.
  5. Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues.
  6. Chameleons take a while to hatch, and don’t live very long.
